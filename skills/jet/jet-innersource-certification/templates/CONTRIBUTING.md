## Starting Contributions

### Contacting the team

When looking to make contributions to the project, the first step is to contact the team. For communication details, please refer to the [Communication](./COMMUNICATION.md) page.

### Alignment Meeting

For significant changes, we will require an alignment meeting which will include our Trusted Committer(s) and Product Manager. Alignment for smaller changes may be able to be agreed offline, through our Slack channel.

### ADRs

We may require you to create an ADR, which will need to be approved before work starts.

### Timelines

Once the approach has been agreed, work can begin at the agreed time, following the guidelines below.

A Trusted Committer will review your PR within 48 hours.

## Post Deployment

If there are any bugs within 30 days, it is up to the contributing team to fix them. When a Trusted Committer contacts you about a bug caused by a change you made, it should take priority for you and your team to fix it. The 30-day warranty period begins once the feature has demonstrated steady traffic and is actively being used.

See the [30-day warranty pattern](https://patterns.innersourcecommons.org/p/30-day-warranty) for details.
